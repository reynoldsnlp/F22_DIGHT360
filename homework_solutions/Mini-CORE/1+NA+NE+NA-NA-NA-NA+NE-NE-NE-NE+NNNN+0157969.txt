<p> WAPELLO COUNTY, Iowa   Car trouble led to a crash this morning near Ottumwa that took the lives of two St. Louis area men, police say.
<p> UPDATED at 6 p.m. with identity of man killed in crash.
<p> HAZELWOOD  Police here are investigating a fatal crash about midnight in the 5800 block of North Lindbergh Boulevard.
<p> At: 11:59 p.m., officers found a car on fire after it had apparently crashed, police say. Firefighters put out the blaze and found a male inside the car.
<p> Authorities this afternoon identified the driver as Victor Gonzalez, 30, of Hazelwood, but did not say what caused the crash.
<p> In a later, unrelated crash, a stolen car fleeing police struck a utility pole at McDonnell Boulevard near North Lindbergh Boulevard, police said. That crash was at about 2:15 a.m.
<p> Hazelwood police Lt. Jim Hudanick said in an email that a Hazelwood officer had followed the vehicle after it struck another officer's squad car at the Knollwood apartment complex in the 5400 block of Knoll Creek Drive, where officers were checking on a report of car break-ins.
<p> Four males were seriously hurt in the crash, Hudanick said. One of the four had life-threatening injuries. All were taken to hospitals for treatment. One has since been released.
