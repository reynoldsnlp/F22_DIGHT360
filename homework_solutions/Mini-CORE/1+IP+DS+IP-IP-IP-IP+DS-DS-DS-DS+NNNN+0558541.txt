<h> AROUND THE WORLD: OUR ANIMALS
<p> People all around the world keep animals for company as pets, for food (milk and meat) and clothing (wool and hair), and for carrying loads. Pigs, donkeys, goats and sheep are just some of the many types of animals that help us. This book, perfect to share with a young child, has large clear photographs of different children and their animals from all around the world. It includes a map of the world indicating the countries the photographs come from. Published in association with Oxfam this series aims to draw parallels between children from many different cultures.
<p> Engaging pictures from highly-talented photographers, shot on location and featuring real children in their day-to-day lives
<p> In collaboration with the charity, OXFAM
<p> Other titles in the Around the World series: At School, Bicycles, Hair, Home, Playtime
<h> About the author
<p> OXFAM works in 77 countries and supports nearly 3,000 long-term development projects. Its publishing division helps raise awareness of life and cultures all over the world.
