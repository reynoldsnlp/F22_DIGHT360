<h> Maple Leaf facility takes shape in Puslinch
<p> Members of the construction crew work on the girders for the roof of the new Maple Leaf Foods distribution centre that is going up at McLean Road and Brock Road in Puslinch Township on Thursday. The building, which will replace distribution centres in Kitchener, Burlington and Moncton, N.B., is expected to open next year.
