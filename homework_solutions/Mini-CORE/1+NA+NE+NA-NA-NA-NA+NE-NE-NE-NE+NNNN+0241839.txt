<h> Sanaa Lathan to Star in By the Way, Meet Vera Stark at Geffen Playhouse
<p> Sanaa Lathan (courtesy of the company) The Geffen Playhouse has announced casting for the West Coast premiere of By the Way, Meet Vera Stark , running September 18-October 28 in the Gil Cates Theater. Jo Bonney will direct.
<p> In By the Way, Meet Vera Stark , former maid Vera Stark lands a groundbreaking role in a new Southern epic and turns Hollywood on its head as she paves the way for future generations of black actresses.
