<p> Welcome to EduCareDo
<h> EduCareDo International
<h> Research and Learning Centre
<h> Based on the work of Rudolf Steiner
<h> EduCareDo is a new way of self awakening and learning.
<h> It evolved in response to the needs of many people who are searching for new possibilities to develop in freedom their human potential on the path of self-knowledge. This path is for self development and consequently for the benefit of fellow human beings and the world.
<h> It is for people, who, due to their life circumstances such as family, work, time restrictions, health, financial difficulties and distances from learning centers cannot attend regular courses, seminars or colleges.
